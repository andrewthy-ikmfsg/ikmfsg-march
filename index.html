What is ICT?

ICT – Integrated Combat Training is Singapore's premier multi-discipline martial arts programme, combining the most effective elements of Krav Maga self-defence, Taekwondo precision, and Muay Thai striking under one certified roof.

Led by Andrew Thien — IKMF-certified Krav Maga instructor, Kukkiwon Black Belt, and Kru Muay Thai instructor — our curriculum is built on practical field-tested techniques that work for real people in real situations.

Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned practitioner, ICT offers a structured path from foundational safety awareness to advanced combat competency across all three disciplines.

Our Martial Arts

Three Disciplines. One Platform.

Each discipline complements the others — building a complete martial artist capable of handling any situation.

Krav Maga

The world's most effective real-world self-defence system. Created by Imi Lichtenfeld, Krav Maga combines techniques from boxing, wrestling, aikido, judo and karate with brutally practical scenario training.

Unlike traditional martial arts, Krav Maga has no rules, no competitions, no kata — only field-tested responses to real threats: weapon attacks, multiple assailants, and high-stress confrontations.

At ICT, our IKMF-certified programme follows the global graded curriculum from Practitioner to Expert levels, covering threat neutralisation, situational awareness, and decisive action under pressure.

Self-Defence Threat Neutralisation Situational Awareness IKMF Graded All Levels

Taekwondo

The art of the foot and fist. Originating in Korea and recognised as an Olympic sport, Taekwondo is renowned for its spectacular kicks, lightning-fast footwork, and deep philosophical traditions rooted in discipline and respect.

Taekwondo develops unparalleled leg strength, flexibility, coordination, and mental focus. Its dynamic striking techniques translate directly into practical self-defence applications and competitive excellence.

Andrew holds a Black Belt certified by the Singapore Taekwondo Federation and Kukkiwon, with multiple podium finishes in competitive tournaments. Our programme covers both patterns (poomsae) and sparring for a complete development path.

Olympic Sport Kukkiwon Certified Poomsae & Sparring Kicks & Footwork Youth & Adults

Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs. Thailand's national combat sport uses fists, elbows, knees, and shins — making it one of the most complete striking systems in the world. Known as the "Science of Eight Limbs," Muay Thai has proven its effectiveness in every major combat sport.

Beyond its competitive applications, Muay Thai delivers elite conditioning, core strength, and full-body power. It is one of the most physically transformative training systems available — and one of the most effective real-world striking arts.

Andrew is a FitMuay Instructor. Our classes cover clinch work, pad work, bag training, and controlled sparring for all experience levels.
