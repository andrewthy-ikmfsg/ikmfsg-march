Our Discipline

What is Krav Maga?

Krav Maga is a practical self-defence system developed for real-world situations, combining techniques from boxing, wrestling, aikido, judo and karate with realistic fight training.

Created by Imi Lichtenfeld, who used his boxing and wrestling background to defend his community in Bratislava during the 1930s, Krav Maga was later refined into a structured self-defence system and eventually adapted for civilian training worldwide.

Unlike traditional martial arts, Krav Maga focuses on real-world scenarios: threat neutralisation, situational awareness, and decisive action under pressure. There are no competitions, no kata — only practical, field-tested techniques.
